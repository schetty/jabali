Our Vision

Masseria Jabali blends premium assisted living with a 3-ha syntropic farm, welcoming 35+ international residents. English-speaking carers and refugee trainees foster an inclusive, supportive community.

We are building a new model for assisted living that is both socially responsible and environmentally regenerative, aiming to set a new standard for eldercare in the Mediterannean.
